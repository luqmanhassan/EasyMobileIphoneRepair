Our History


The Easy Mobile Iphone Repair story began in 2019 with—what else?—a broken phone. Founder Luqman Hakeem’s iPhone took a topple and shattered, and the then college student decided to fix it, rather than pay. By 2019, Hakeem’s resourcefulness and skill at fixing iPhones had spread beyond his friends and family, and he formed Easy Mobile Iphone Repair out his grandmas living room, fixing client’s phones on the spot.

By 2020, the company had grown to serve over five appointments a day, moving to retail premises located in Tukwila. Easy Mobile Iphone Repair continued to expand rapidly, moving yet again, this time to its flagship location on Renton.

To date, Easy Mobile Iphone Repair has fixed over 1,000 devices and gained a loyal customer base. The company’s story is far from over, with new chapters already created with the opening of our Dallas location.
